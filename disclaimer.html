The purpose of this module is to ensure that there is diversity and inclusion in workplaces. By the end of this module, you will have improved employee engagement skills. It helps you build stronger, healthier relationships with peers and colleagues.

This module starts with a short survey which asks a few questions about your background. This information is used to determine what kind of content you see in this course. This information we get from you is completely anonymous and confidential. You can exit the module at any point if you are sensitive to this training due to past experiences or any other reasons or if you are not willing to share your information with us. In the duration of this module, if there is any section/question you are not comfortable with, feel free to skip that section/question.

If you are willing to begin, click on start survey to begin.
